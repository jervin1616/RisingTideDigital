The Origin

A Legacy of Grit & Logic

My fascination with how things work didn't start in a classroom. It started on the floor of my mother's Audio Video Repair shop, watching her take apart high-end electronics, sewing machines, and household appliances with the same calm focus. She didn't send things out. She figured them out. From her, I learned that most problems aren't mysterious — they just haven't been looked at closely enough.

My father took a different angle. He spent years running Internet departments for major car dealerships, showing traditional businesses how to show up and compete in a digital world. He understood that a strong online presence wasn't a luxury — it was the difference between being found and being invisible.

I grew up between those two mindsets. That's still how I work today.

"Build it right. Put it where people can find it."

The Why

Built for a Purpose

I started Rising Tide Digital because I kept seeing the same problem: hardworking local business owners losing ground to competitors who just had a better website. Not better service. Not more experience. Just a more professional digital presence.

That bothered me. It still does.

I had the skills to fix it, and I knew the kind of people who needed it — contractors, cleaners, handymen, service providers who are excellent at what they do but aren't supposed to also be web designers. Rising Tide Digital exists to close that gap.
