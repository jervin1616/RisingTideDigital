How It Works

From Idea to Live Site

A simple, transparent 5-step process — no confusion, no surprises, no agency runaround.

01

Free Consultation

Tell us about your business, your goals, and what you need. No pressure, no commitment. We ask the right questions to understand exactly what will work best for you.

02

Custom Quote

We send a clear, flat-rate proposal — no hourly billing, no hidden costs. You know exactly what you're getting and what it costs before we start a single line of code.

03

Design & Build

Pay your 50% deposit to kick things off. We design and build your site while keeping you in the loop — you'll never wonder what's happening or when it'll be done.

04

Review & Revise

You review the site, request changes, and we refine until it's exactly right. Revisions are included in every package — we don't move forward until you're genuinely happy.

05

Launch

Final payment, domain pointed, site goes live. Your business is officially online — and we stick around to make sure everything runs smoothly on launch day.

Flat-Rate, No Surprises

Every project is quoted upfront. 50% starts the build — the rest is due only when you're ready to launch.

Direct Owner Access

You work with Jervin directly the entire time — not an account manager or offshore team.

You Own Everything

Your code, your domain, your files — all handed to you at launch. No platform lock-in, ever.

Revisions Included

Every package includes revision rounds. We refine until you're happy — that's the deal.
