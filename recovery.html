The Why

Why I Built This

I got sober on October 26, 2021. I have been to multiple treatment programs and sober living homes. The hardest part was finding them. Most recovery organizations are doing incredible work — but they are invisible online. I am a CADC-I and I work in a non-medical social model detox facility. I built One Day Digital because I know what it feels like to be on the other side of that search.
